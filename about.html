The Beginning

Brewed from
an Obsession.

ReBrew started from curiosity more than a business plan. I always wanted to create something that felt different from regular soft drinks — something with more character, more depth, and more of a story behind it.

What began as small experiments with fruits, fermentation, and flavour combinations slowly turned into an obsession with building a unique beverage experience.

The early days were filled with trial batches, failed experiments, recipe changes, and learning how fermentation behaves over time. Every bottle taught something new.

Instead of creating another sugary soda, the goal was to build a handcrafted fruit brew that felt refreshing, naturally alive, and slightly different every time you opened it.

That idea eventually became ReBrew.

"To create a drink that feels different."

From designing the first labels and testing flavours to supplying cafés, restaurants, and retail shelves, the journey has been built one step at a time — with consistency, experimentation, and the belief that people are always looking for something new to discover.

Today, ReBrew continues to grow across stores, restaurants, and events, while staying true to the same vision it started with.

The Journey

From Batch 001
to Today.

Oct 2025

First Experiment

Kitchen-scale fermentation tests begin. Failed batches, exploding bottles, and a growing obsession with fruit fermentation.

Nov 2025

The First Label

First production-ready batch. Label design finalized. Grape and Ginger debut at a local market — sells out in 2 hours.

Dec 2025

Café Partnerships

First café orders land. Apple & Cinnamon and Pineapple flavours added. Growing word-of-mouth in Coimbatore.

Jan 2026

Retail & Events

ReBrew lands on retail shelves. Festival pop-ups begin. Mint flavour launches. Brand identity refined and production scaled.

2026

Growing the Dream

Expanding across South India. New flavours in development. Restaurant and hospitality partnerships deepening. The story continues.
