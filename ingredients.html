The Foundation

Every Bottle
Starts Here.

💧

Filtered Water

The Base

The foundation of every brew. We use filtered, mineral-balanced water to ensure consistent fermentation and a clean, neutral base for our fruits to shine.

🍑

Real Fruit

The Character

We use actual fruit — grapes, apples, pineapple, ginger root — not concentrates, not flavouring, not extracts. The fruit is the brew.

🌾

Cane Sugar

The Fuel

A small amount of unrefined cane sugar feeds the fermentation process. Most of it gets consumed by the cultures — what remains is minimal and natural.

🦠

Live Cultures

The Magic

Natural fermentation cultures — yeast and beneficial bacteria — create the carbonation, develop the complexity, and give ReBrew its alive, wild character.

⏳

Time

The Secret

You can't rush fermentation. Time is what transforms simple ingredients into something with depth, complexity, and a story. We never shortcut this.

🌿

Spices & Herbs

The Accent

For Apple & Cinnamon and Mint varieties, we add real cinnamon bark and fresh mint leaves. Nothing powdered. Nothing artificial.

What's in

Grape Brew

Deep & Wild

"Rich with character. Tart. Alive."

🍇

Fresh Concord Grapes

Dark, tannic, full-bodied grapes that ferment beautifully into a rich, complex base.

💧

Filtered Water

Mineral-balanced and neutral to support healthy fermentation.

🌾

Unrefined Cane Sugar

Feeds the fermentation. Minimal residual sugar in the final product.

🦠

Natural Fermentation Cultures

Wild yeast and beneficial bacteria that create carbonation and complexity.

Apple & Cinnamon Brew

Warm & Spiced

"Autumn in a bottle."

🍎

Fresh Apples

Crisp, tart orchard apples fermented to develop natural sweetness and depth.

🪵

Aged Cinnamon Bark

Real Sri Lankan cinnamon bark — not powder — steeped through the fermentation process.

💧

Filtered Water

Clean and balanced to carry the warm spice profile.

🦠

Natural Fermentation Cultures

Wild yeast and bacteria doing their slow, beautiful work.

Ginger Brew

Sharp & Alive

"Wakes you up from the first sip."

🫚

Fresh Ginger Root

Whole ginger root — pressed and steeped for maximum heat and natural flavour.

🍋

Lemon Juice

A small amount of real lemon juice adds brightness and supports fermentation pH.

💧

Filtered Water

Neutral base for a clean, sharp ginger expression.

🦠

Natural Fermentation Cultures

Creates the natural fizz and wild character of a true ginger brew.

Pineapple Brew

Tropical & Light

"Sun-ripened. Naturally alive."

🍍

Fresh Pineapple

Whole pineapples — including core — fermented for tropical depth and natural sweetness.

💧

Filtered Water

Light and clean to let the pineapple character lead.

🌾

Unrefined Cane Sugar

Minimal — mostly consumed during fermentation to create natural carbonation.

🦠

Natural Fermentation Cultures

Creates the effervescence that makes every sip feel alive.

Mint Brew

Cool & Herbal

"The cleanest sip in the collection."

🌿

Fresh Spearmint Leaves

Hand-picked spearmint, steeped through fermentation for a clean, cool herbal character.

🍋

Lime Juice

A dash of fresh lime juice brightens the mint and adds tartness to the finish.

💧

Filtered Water

Ultra-clean water to let the herbal notes shine without interference.

🦠

Natural Fermentation Cultures

The gentle fermentation creates a soft, refreshing fizz that complements mint perfectly.
