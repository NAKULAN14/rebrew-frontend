Got Questions?

FAQ.

"We've got answers."

What is ReBrew?

+

ReBrew is a premium handcrafted fermented fruit soda — naturally carbonated, 0.0% alcohol, made with real fruit and no artificial additives. Each bottle is a small-batch brew with its own unique character.

What flavours are available?

+

We currently offer five flavours: Grape, Apple & Cinnamon, Ginger, Pineapple, and Mint. Each is brewed separately and has its own distinct tasting notes and character.

How much is in each bottle?

+

Each ReBrew bottle is 275ml — the perfect serving size. One bottle, one story.

How long is the shelf life?

+

ReBrew has a shelf life of 90 days from the bottling date. Because it's naturally fermented with no preservatives, it's best consumed fresh. Store in a cool, dark place and refrigerate after opening.

What does "naturally fermented" mean?

+

Natural fermentation means we use real fruit and time — no artificial carbonation or forced fermentation. The bubbles you taste are produced naturally by the fermentation process itself. This gives ReBrew its alive, wild character.

Does ReBrew contain alcohol?

+

No. ReBrew is 0.0% alcohol. The fermentation process is carefully controlled to keep it fully non-alcoholic, making it suitable for everyone including children and those who abstain from alcohol.

Why does each bottle taste slightly different?

+

That's the magic of small-batch fermentation. Natural processes create slight variations between batches — in carbonation level, tartness, and flavour depth. This is a feature, not a flaw. Every bottle genuinely has its own story.

What is "Bottled Alive" mean on the label?

+

Bottled Alive means we seal each bottle at the peak of active fermentation, capturing live cultures and natural carbonation inside. The brew continues its subtle fermentation journey even in the bottle — hence, it's alive.

Do you ship across India?

+

Yes! We deliver pan-India. Shipping is free on orders above ₹1000. For orders below ₹1000, a flat shipping fee of ₹40 applies.

How long does delivery take?

+

Standard delivery is 3–5 business days across India. Orders from Coimbatore and nearby areas may arrive faster. You'll receive a tracking link once your order is dispatched.

How do you ensure bottles don't break during shipping?

+

All bottles are individually bubble-wrapped and packed in secure corrugated boxes. If anything arrives damaged, contact us within 24 hours with a photo and we'll replace it, no questions asked.

Is ReBrew good for gut health?

+

Naturally fermented drinks have been traditionally associated with gut-friendly benefits due to the presence of natural cultures. While ReBrew isn't a medical product, it's made without artificial additives, preservatives, or excess sugar — making it a far healthier choice than conventional sodas.

Can children drink ReBrew?

+

ReBrew is 0.0% alcohol and made with natural ingredients. However, as it's a fermented product with some tartness and natural cultures, we recommend it for ages 12 and above. When in doubt, consult with your physician.

How much sugar is in ReBrew?

+

ReBrew uses only the natural sugars present in fruit, which are partially consumed during fermentation. There's no added refined sugar, corn syrup, or artificial sweeteners. Exact nutritional info per flavour is available on our packaging.

Can I stock ReBrew at my café or restaurant?

+

Absolutely. We actively partner with cafés, restaurants, hotels, and retail stores. Reach out to us at hello.rebrew@gmail.com or via our contact page with your details and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

Is there a minimum order for wholesale?

+

Yes. Wholesale orders start at a minimum of 24 bottles per order. We offer competitive pricing, dedicated support, and point-of-sale materials for our trade partners.
